About KOSEN-KMITL

The KOSEN-KMITL program represents a partnership between King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang (KMITL) and the Japanese KOSEN (National Institute of Technology) system.

Our program focuses on engineering education, hands-on practice, and innovation to prepare students for real-world careers in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.
